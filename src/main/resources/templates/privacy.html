Privacy & Policy

we protect your data with advanced security and full transparency.

  1. Home
  2. Policy

Our PhoneBook application respects your privacy and understands how important your personal information is. We are fully committed to protecting any data you provide while using our service. Whether you're adding a single contact or managing an entire contact list, your information is stored with care, and we follow strict digital security practices to keep it safe.

When you sign up or use our app, we may collect only the necessary information to help you use PhoneBook effectively. This includes your name, email address, and any contact details you choose to store in your personal account. You are always in control of your own data — we do not access, sell, or share your contact list with anyone, unless required by law.

Security & Storage

  • All contact entries, whether personal or professional, are stored securely in a cloud database.
  • We use encrypted connections, strong access protection, and verified user logins to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Technical information like IP address and browser type may be collected to improve performance.
  • None of this data is linked to your personal identity and is used only to monitor how the system is functioning.
  • We do not allow access to your contact list by any other users or third-party systems without your permission.
  • If you delete your account, your data is permanently removed after a short recovery period.

Your activity data is never shared with third parties for advertising. We may send you updates on features or policy changes, but you can opt out of promotional messages anytime. Our team regularly updates the system to prevent any threats, and backups are taken regularly.

We respect global privacy laws like GDPR. You can request a copy of your data, update it, or delete it permanently. All sharing features are optional and clearly marked.

PhoneBook uses trusted third-party services like cloud providers under strict agreements that ensure your data remains private. By using our service, you agree to our privacy practices.

Please revisit this policy from time to time. Your trust matters, and we're always here to answer your questions about how we handle your data.

We’re committed to giving you full control and transparency over your data. If you ever have questions, our support team is available to assist.

Your Rights

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Update or correct any information
  • Delete data permanently at any time
  • Opt out of communications or notifications
  • Get help from our support team regarding privacy concerns
